# Logo Jury Verdict — Elon Gains

**Date:** 2026-06-30  
**Context:** Menu bar utility tracking portfolio gains; icons must read at 16px and feel native on macOS.

## Candidates

| ID | Concept | Description |
|----|---------|-------------|
| **A** | Chart Line | Light gray squircle, bold green-to-red gradient uptrend line, faint grid, green peak dot |
| **B** | Candlestick Stack | Black bg, three gray-to-green candlesticks increasing height, menu-bar base |
| **C** | Orbit Sparkline | Black bg, blue elliptical ring, zigzag green/red line, white satellite dot |

## Scores

| Criterion | A — Chart Line | B — Candlesticks | C — Orbit Sparkline |
|-----------|----------------|------------------|---------------------|
| Menu bar (16px) | **9** | 7 | 5 |
| macOS native feel | **10** | 6 | 5 |
| Brand fit | **9** | 8 | 7 |
| Distinctiveness | 7 | **9** | 8 |
| App Store | **9** | 7 | 6 |
| **Total** | **44** | **37** | **31** |

## Verdict

**WINNER: A (Chart Line)** — shipped as v2 (dark charcoal bg, no grid)

**RUNNER-UP: B (Candlesticks)** — alternate brand direction in `design/logo-candidates/`

**ELIMINATED: C (Orbit Sparkline)** — too busy at menu-bar size; blue accent clashes with system UI

## v2 revision (applied)

1. Dark charcoal background for light/dark menu bar contrast
2. Grid removed
3. Thicker uptrend stroke retained
4. Green peak dot kept as accent anchor
